Privacy Policy

Sword Drill — by Don Babcock

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Overview

Sword Drill is a Bible reading app that loads passages from Bible Gateway. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains what data the app does — and does not — collect.

Data We Collect

None. Sword Drill does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server:

  • Recent passages — the five most recently viewed passage references, for quick access.
  • Settings — your preferred Bible translation buttons and URL template.
  • Screen-awake preference — whether you have enabled the keep-screen-awake option.

This data is stored using the system's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults) and can be removed by deleting the app.

Network Requests

Sword Drill makes network requests only to Bible Gateway (biblegateway.com) to load passage content. The app applies content-blocking rules that filter out advertisements, tracking scripts, and non-passage content from loaded pages. No data is sent to any other third party.

No Accounts or Login

Sword Drill does not require or support user accounts, login, or registration of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Sword Drill does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
